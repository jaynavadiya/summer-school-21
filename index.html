About IEEE

IEEE Student Branch DAIICT aims to provide an interactive and informative platform for students to develop professional and technical abilities. IEEE SB DAIICT hosts Summer School every year to provide students an opportunity to develop their technical skills and make their summer break a productive one.

This time we bring to you a 5 day-long, Summer School 2021. This summer school aims at providing a hands-on experience in one of the emerging technical domains followed by a competition and exciting prizes to grab.

The domain for Summer School 2021 is Data Science and Machine Learning.


The event will take place on:

4th, 5th and 6th June 2021 - Data Science and Machine Learning Hands-on

12th and 13th June 2021- Advanced Machine Learning Algorithms and Industrial Project



About Data Science & Machine Learning

  • Data Science is a broad and interdisciplinary field using extensive amount of data to gain knowledge and insights. Although data science includes machine learning, it is a vast field with many different tools.
  • Machine learning is a group of techniques used to allow computers to resourcefully learn from available data. Machine learning algorithms operate without explicit rules.
  • Both Data science and machine learning are trendy buzzwords these days. This summer school is going to bring something that will help students gain an overview of Machine Learning dealing with Introduction, carrying on with Statistics, Data analysis and Supervised learning. To conclude the session the students will also be assigned an industry level project to put these new skills to use.

Workshop Details And Benefits

  • Interaction with IT professionals and develop skills related to Data Science and Machine Learning.
  • Interactive Live Lectures with Hands on experience.
  • Assignments during the week break will help students understand the algorithms better and engage their mind in finding solutions to the assigned problem.
  • A project as a conclusion to the session wherein you will focus on the applications of all the algorithms you have learnt so far in the session.
  • Letter of Participation to all the registered candidates.
  • Prerequisites: There are no prerequisites for this workshop.
