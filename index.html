About IEEE

IEEE Student Branch DAIICT aims to provide an interactive and informative platform for students to develop professional and technical abilities. IEEE SB DAIICT hosts Summer School every year to provide students an opportunity to develop their technical skills and make their summer break a productive one.

This time we bring to you a 5 day-long, Summer School 2021.This summer school aims at providing a hands-on experience in one of the emerging technical domains followed by numerous competitions and exciting prizes to grab.

The domain for Summer School 2021 is Artificial Intelligence and Developing a ChatBot.

The event will take place from 2nd June to 6th June, 2021.

About AI & ChatBot

  • Chatbots are all around us today, from ordering food to booking flight tickets.
  • But what is AI & ChatBot?
  • AI is a technology that allows a computer, software or a machine to manifest and 'react' with having qualities similar to human intelligence.
  • A chatbot is an Artificial Intelligence (AI) software which conducts an on-line chat conversation, written or spoken human speech. It is used for customer service, request routing and/or information gathering. Chatbots are often described as one of the most advanced and promising expressions of interaction between humans and machines. Chatbots are all around us today, from ordering food to booking flight tickets.
  • This workshop will bring light on how conversational agents work under the hood.

Workshop Details And Benefits

  • Interaction with IT professionals and develop skills on AI, ML and ChatBot concepts.
  • PowerPoint Presentations and Interactive Live Lectures with Hands on experience.
  • A contest to conclude the session wherein you will focus on the practical applications of what you have learnt.
  • Prerequisites: There are no prerequisites for this workshop as we will cover everything from scratch.
